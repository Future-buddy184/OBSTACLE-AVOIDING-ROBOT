## Budget Notes

- The original design used an L298N motor driver.
- Hardware testing revealed power efficiency and reliability limitations.
- The project was upgraded to a TB6612FNG motor driver.
- The power system is also being upgraded from 4×AA batteries to two 18650 Li-ion cells for better performance.
